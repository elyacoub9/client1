Our Story

Founded in 2015, Jakom began as a small corner shop with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a welcoming space. Today, we've grown into a community hub where coffee lovers, students, and professionals gather to connect and create.

We're passionate about ethically sourced beans, expertly roasted to bring out their unique flavors. Every drink is crafted with care by our trained baristas.

Localisation

Find us in the heart of the city, nestled between historic buildings and modern architecture. Our café is easily accessible by public transport and offers street parking nearby.

Address

123 Coffee Street, Cityville

Getting Here

By Metro: Take the Blue Line to Cityville Central Station, then walk 5 minutes north on Coffee Street.

By Bus: Routes 12, 24, and 36 stop within one block of our location.

Parking: Street parking is available on Coffee Street and nearby side streets. A paid parking garage is located two blocks away on Market Street.
